The Task

Our task was to design and implement a game for a console resembling the Nintendo Gameboy. The console should also have a sound output for music and sound effects.

Hardware

The console features two microcontrollers:

  • Atmega328 with
    • 32 KiB flash
    • 2 KiB SRAM
    • 1 KiB EEPROM
  • Atmega168 with
    • 16 KiB flash
    • 1 KiB SRAM
    • 512 B EEPROM

where the former is responsible for the game logic and controlling the display and the latter is used solely as a sound controller.

Furthermore, it features an EA DOGXL160L-7. This LC display has a resolution of 160Ã—104 pixels, but four vertically adjacent pixels are grouped together in so-called pages and can only be written as a whole. Therefore, when writing to the display, the x coordinate must be below 160 and the y coordinate below 25.

Last but not least, the 8 buttons known from the Gameboy are available:

  • left, right, up, down
  • A, B
  • pause, select
