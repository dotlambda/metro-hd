About

This is the result of an advanced practical at the robotics lab Heidelberg, accomplished in the summer semester 2017. Its goal was the implementation of a game on a microcontroller-based game console.

Project Evolution

The practical began in May 2017 and ended in September. Until mid-July, we familiarized with Atmega programming and decided on a game. Then, we implemented a first basic version until the end of July, which was refined in August. Finally, we added sound effects and background music in September.
